About Me


Hello! My name is Cressida and I’m a junior front-end developer based in Somerset, UK. Since I was a young girl, I have always enjoyed arts & crafts and found great pleasure in creative undertakings. As an adult, it was only natural that I wanted to try something that would challenge that side of me once more.


At the start of 2023, I made myself a New Year’s resolution to learn something new and that is how I found myself in the world of technology. A far cry from the world of farming that I am used to! I am so glad I did challenge myself to do it though. Since doing so, I have discovered a love for the world of web development and the beauty behind creating websites and applications like the ones you can see in my portfolio.
